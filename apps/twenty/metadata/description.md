Twenty is a modern, open-source CRM designed for companies that want full control over their customer relationship data.

## Features

- **Pipeline Management** — Visual kanban boards to track deals through stages
- **Contact & Company Tracking** — Rich profiles with activity timeline, notes, and tasks
- **Task Automation** — Automate repetitive workflows with built-in triggers and actions
- **API-First** — GraphQL and REST APIs for deep integrations with your existing tools
- **Custom Objects** — Extend the data model with custom fields and objects to match your business
- **Email Integration** — Connect your email to log conversations automatically
- **Open Source** — Self-hosted with full data ownership, no vendor lock-in

## Why Twenty?

Twenty provides a Notion-like experience for CRM. Instead of being locked into Salesforce or HubSpot, you own your data and can customize every aspect of the platform. The modern UI is built with React and the backend runs on Node.js with PostgreSQL.
